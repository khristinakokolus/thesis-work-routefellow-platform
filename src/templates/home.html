ABOUT US

RouteFellow is an application that will help you in trip planning. It considers two important things as price and time on transportation.
Using this tool, you can save time on trip planning and take time only to consider search criteria. As an output you will get possible
flight and bus tickets to get to your destinations.

You should consider such main search requirements as:

  • City from where you want to depart
  • Cities that you want to visit
  • Amount of days you want to spend in each city
  • Date of departure
  • Date of arrival back to departure city
  • Maximum price of the transportation in your trip
  • Maximum amount of transfer changes during transportation between two cities; 0 - transportations with no stops, 1 - allowed tickets with 1 transfer change, etc.
  • Number of adults that want to travel
  • Transport options you want to travel with
